DVD Series
by Nicholas Braica

Adobe InDesign + Adobe Photoshop

A DVD series done for a graphic design course at Northeastern.

My Task
To take four classic films and create film covers for each, with the idea being that the four would fit into a stylistic series. The whole project took on many revisions and style changes, all of which are documented in a processbook done for the entirety of the course.
