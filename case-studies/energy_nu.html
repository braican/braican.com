Flash + Actionscript 3.0 + HTML

EnergyNU is a project from the Sustainability department at Northeastern University. The idea was to have a fully interactive site that reports on different sustainability-related items for several buildings on campus, including LEED scorecard and status, energy and water consumption, and other building information.

My Task
To make an attractive splash page for users to navigate to the information of their choice. The page would include a map of Northeastern University's campus, with the actual building images (as well as their labels) hyperlinked to the in-detail page for that building.

Adobe Photoshop was used to create the "hover" images for the buildings, and Flash was used to place and align the map and all of its parts. Actionscript 3.0 made the whole interactive experience, dictating the hover events as well as the click events.

Visit EnergyNU.neu.edu/energynu/
