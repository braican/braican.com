HTML + CSS + WordPress

Green Innovations is a company started by Andrea Putman, a colleague of mine when I worked for Second Nature. The business provides expertise on innovative best practices to transition academic institutions toward new-zero energy campuses profitably.

My Task
To develop the branding for the company, including creating a clear identity mark, a business card, and an attractive, informative website. The site needed to be easily editable by the client, who wanted the ability to add publications, clients, and other basic pages and content for the website. For this reason, I opted for the simplicity of WordPress.
