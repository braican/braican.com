HTML + CSS + WordPress

The Boston Blues are the official supporters group of the Chelsea Football club of the Premiere League, representing Boston and the rest of New England.

My Task
To design and develop a site that would allow members of the group to easily populate and edit content. A news section pulling RSS feeds from several different soccer (or football, if you prefer) sources was also desireable.

Simplicity was paramount in this case, so using WordPress as a CMS was logical. Users are able to edit the several sections with ease, and the extendability may come in handy for any future updates or expansions of the site. Two plugins (FeedWordPress and Auto Prune Posts) were utilized to syndicate feeds from five different sources and to delete the posts after getting too old.
