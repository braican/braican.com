Drawing and painting. And all that other really artsy stuff.

To be perfectly honest, I'm not a big fan of the more traditional art mediums, like drawing, painting, sculpture, stuff like that. However, they are the foundations of any artistic path, and I did have a good time creating the work shown here. The majority of the work displayed was done earlier in my life, specifically high school, but exemplifies that idea of how everything I've done has led to my present work and future direction.

As there is quite a bit of work here, the pieces are further categorized by the specific medium. Click on the thumbnail for a larger view, and a brief description of the piece.
