You know that saying about quality and quantity? Where does progress fall under those categories? My portfolio includes almost everything I've done artistically; from 8th grade to college work, from music to painting. And that's because everything I've created and been able to do, up to this point, has influenced what I can create and do today. Everything on this website shows a different part of how I think, how I behave, and most importantly, where I've come from as a designer, artist, programmer, or whatever I am. It's all relevant to my development and future progress.

So take a look around. More importantly, enjoy. There's a decent amount of work here, and of all different mediums, interactivity levels, and subjects. And yes, some of it is somewhat goofy. But hey, we all had to start somewhere.
